Title: CompEx Ex05-Ex06 Combustible Dust

Short Summary

Gain the skills you need for electrical installation, maintenance and inspection to IEC 60079-14. Designed for those working in potentially explosive environments due to dust, this course will help you understand the general principles of explosion protection, types of protection available and relevant markings. The presenter will also explain the aspects of equipment design which affect protection concept, and the associated standards. This course consists of classroom based theoretical training, practical assessment and a written examination. Delegates will have the opportunity to get hands on and assemble equipment (such as glands).

Description

Who Should Attend?
Anyone who conducts electrical installation or inspection of hazardous dust areas, normally practitioners with an electrical installation background.

Topics Covered

Certificates Issued?
National certificate for hazardous locations

Prerequisites

NVQ Level 3 in Electrical Engineering, JIB Card with Electrical Qualification or a Certificate of Apprenticeship in Electrical Engineering. Photo Identification is required.
